About Sacred Heart Academy

Sacred Heart Academy is a traditional Catholic school located in Burlington, Colorado, operated by the Sisters of the Mother of God (CMD). We offer a full K–12 traditional education deeply rooted in the Catholic faith, and the Traditional Latin Mass.

Our dedicated teachers and religious sisters guide students in the pursuit of wisdom, virtue, and knowledge — forming young men and women who are prepared to serve God, their families, and society with integrity and purpose.

We welcome families seeking a faithful, academically rigorous alternative to modern education. Our school is small by design, allowing every student to receive personal attention and to grow in a truly Catholic environment.
